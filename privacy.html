Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, particularly when you fill out our workflow audit intake form. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us, but typically includes corporate email addresses, names, and business operational data.

We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our services, communicate with you, and for security and fraud prevention. Specifically, data submitted via our intake portal is routed securely through Make.com and Formspree solely for internal evaluation and to generate your custom ROI proposal.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data to third parties. We only share information with explicit consent, to comply with laws, or with essential service providers necessary to operate our business infrastructure (e.g., Stripe for payment processing, Calendly for scheduling, and cloud hosting providers).

4. Data Retention and Security

We keep your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy notice unless otherwise required by law. We have implemented appropriate and reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it. To request to review, update, or delete your personal information, please submit a direct request via our primary contact channels.
